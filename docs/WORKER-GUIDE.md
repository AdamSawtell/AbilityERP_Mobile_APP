# AbilityERP Worker App — Usage Guide

For support workers using the AbilityERP mobile app on their phone.

**Live app:** https://main.d3ec4nkn82ouib.amplifyapp.com

---

## Install on your phone

### iPhone (Safari)

1. Open the app link in **Safari** (not Chrome).
2. Tap **Share** (square with arrow).
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Tap **Add**.

The app opens full-screen like a native app — no browser bar.

### Android (Chrome)

1. Open the app link in **Chrome**.
2. Tap the menu (⋮) or the **Install app** banner if shown.
3. Tap **Install** or **Add to Home screen**.

---

## Sign in

- **Username & password** — same login as AbilityERP (e.g. your staff username).
- **Sign in with Microsoft** — if your organisation uses Microsoft 365 SSO.

You must have the **Support Worker** role in AbilityERP. If login fails, contact your rostering team.

---

## Bottom navigation

| Tab | What it does |
|-----|----------------|
| **Open** | Shifts you can apply for (this and next pay fortnight) |
| **Schedule** | Your confirmed rostered shifts |
| **Roster** | Your master roster pattern (Day 1–14 recurring template) |
| **ID** | Digital worker ID card |
| **Leave** | View leave and submit requests |
| **Profile** | Your contact details |

---

## Available Shifts (Open)

- Shows open shifts for **this fortnight** and **next fortnight** (use the tabs at the top).
- Tap **Apply for shift** to request a shift.
- After applying, the shift stays visible with an **Applied** badge — your rostering officer reviews it in AbilityERP.
- Applying does **not** automatically roster you; approval happens in the office.

**Statuses on shift cards:**

| Badge | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| Open | You can apply |
| Applied | You have requested this shift — pending approval |
| Assigned | You are confirmed on the shift |
| Unavailable | Another worker is already assigned |

---

## My Schedule

- Confirmed shifts only (not pending applications).
- Same fortnight tabs as Available Shifts.
- Read-only — to change availability use Leave or contact rostering.

---

## Master Roster

- Your recurring **Day 1 to Day 14** pattern from AbilityERP.
- Sorted by day, then start time within each day.
- Read-only reference for your usual hours.

---

## Leave

- View existing leave entries.
- Submit new leave / unavailability requests (subject to your organisation’s approval workflow in AbilityERP).

---

## Profile & ID

- **Profile** — view and update contact details where allowed.
- **ID** — digital worker ID for showing on site.

---

## Tips

- Pull down or navigate away and back to refresh shift lists after applying.
- If shifts look empty, check you are on the correct fortnight tab (current vs next).
- Use **Add to Home Screen** for the best experience — faster launch and full-screen layout.

---

## Need help?

Contact your rostering officer or system administrator. Mention **AbilityERP Worker App** and the screen you were on (e.g. Available Shifts, Schedule).
